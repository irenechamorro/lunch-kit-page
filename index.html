CHILDREN'S LUNCH KIT

This product design project was created to pratice using Rhinoceros 3D modeling software. It

is an interactive children lunch kit that combines a puzzle piece lunch box and rainbow lights

water bottle. Both products are for creating a fun interesting lunch experience that make it

easier for kids to finish their food and properly hydrate, and for parents to track how much

their children are eating and drinking.

The purpose of this water bottle is to insure that students are drinking enough water throughout the

day, as research has shown that children ages 9-12 should drink about 50 ounces of water daily.

Every time the bottle is refilled a sensor at the top of the bottle indicates this, and one of the

lights turns on, programmed to do so by a small arduino located at the base of the bottle. At the

end of the day, if the 10 ounce bottle is refilled 5 times, the whole rainbow should be lit up. The

rainbow challenge makes kids excited about drinking water and refilling the bottle enough times

to turn on all the colored lights. This also shows parents exactly how much water their children are

drinking per day.
